Holistic Applied Positive Psychology International

What is it?

HAPPI is a membership group for positive psychology students and practitioners.

For students and professionals we're a group for connecting, sharing, and improving your practice, through networking and peer-support. We are run by and for our members who are all students and professionals in the field.

For employers we offer an up-to-date register of positive psychology professionals shown to uphold the very best in education, experience, and ethics.
